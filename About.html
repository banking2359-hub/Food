About Delivery Bdu

Connecting Students with Delicious Food Since 2025

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable food delivery services to students across Bahir Dar University, ensuring they can focus on their studies while enjoying quality meals.

Our Vision

To become the leading student-focused food delivery platform in Ethiopia, revolutionizing how students access food on campus and beyond.

Our Story

Delivery Bdu was founded in 2025 by Bahir Dar University students Biniyam G/slassie who faced daily challenge of finding quality, affordable food between classes and study sessions.

What started as a small project to connect local restaurants with students has grown into a comprehensive food delivery service serving hundreds of students daily.

We partner with the best local restaurants, traditional eateries, and international cuisine providers to bring you diverse food options at student-friendly prices.

Our Core Values
